## 1. Local business owner personas

Shared traits: time-poor, often on mobile between jobs, burned by agencies, wants calls/appointments/reputation, low patience for tools.

| Persona | Goals | Pain points | Marketing maturity | Tech comfort | Ideal dashboard | Ideal onboarding | Ideal communication |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Contractor** | More local jobs; look professional on Google | No time; inconsistent posting; review replies lag | Low–medium | Low | Health + “approve 3 items” | Connect GBP fast; skip jargon | SMS/email weekly; short |
| **Dentist / clinic** | Trust + new patients | Compliance caution; brand tone matters | Medium | Medium | Health + reputation | Brand voice early | Email; polished tone |
| **Restaurant** | Busy nights; seasonal menus | Hours/photos drift; reviews spike | Medium | Medium | Health + reviews emphasis | Photos + hours first | Fast mobile approvals |
| **Landscaper** | Seasonal demand capture | Feast/famine messaging | Low | Low | Health + seasonal next step | Service areas + season | Weekly package |
| **Law office** | Credible local presence | Risk-averse copy | Medium–high | Medium | Health + careful approvals | Voice + compliance notes | Email; Hands-On default |
| **Insurance agency** | Local discoverability vs competitors | Comparison shopping online | Medium | Medium | Health + GBP focus | Competitors + GBP | Weekly Manager |

## 2. Online business owner personas

Shared traits: more digital-native, still hate tool sprawl; want consistency and clear outcomes.

| Persona | Goals | Pain points | Marketing maturity | Tech comfort | Ideal dashboard | Ideal onboarding | Ideal communication |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Coach / consultant** | Authority + inbound | Inconsistent content | Medium | Medium–high | Health + content rhythm | Brand voice + audience | Weekly email |
| **SaaS founder** | Pipeline + trust | No time for “marketing ops” | Medium–high | High | Health + results narrative | Lightweight; API-minded later | Exception-based over time |

## 3. End-to-end journeys

For each stage: emotion → goal → questions → messaging → magic moments → success.

### 3.1 Public website
- **Emotion:** Skeptical hope  
- **Goal:** Understand if this removes marketing burden  
- **Questions:** What is this? Who is it for? Do I stay in control? What’s next?  
- **Messaging:** Head of Marketing / peace of mind — not “AI platform”  
- **Magic:** Interactive demo shows “what we’d do for *my* business”  
- **Success:** Starts demo or signup without confusion  

### 3.2 Signup
- **Emotion:** Mild commitment anxiety  
- **Goal:** Create account safely  
- **Questions:** Is my data safe? What am I signing up for?  
- **Messaging:** Calm, short; link Privacy/Terms when available  
- **Magic:** “Welcome — I’m already ready to learn your business.”  
- **Success:** Lands in onboarding, not empty dashboard chaos  

### 3.3 Onboarding
- **Emotion:** “Please don’t make this homework”  
- **Goal:** Connect the minimum that unlocks value  
- **Questions:** How long? What do you need from me?  
- **Messaging:** “I’m learning about your business.” Progressive steps.  
- **Magic:** “Go run your business — I’ll take it from here.”  
- **Success:** First analysis/profile/plan path started; GBP path clear  

### 3.4 First login
- **Emotion:** Orientation  
- **Goal:** Know where to look  
- **Questions:** Is anything broken? What do I do?  
- **Messaging:** Health + one next step; hide internal feature sprawl  
- **Magic:** Checklist that finishes into calm home  
- **Success:** Completes one meaningful action or sees “you’re set”  

### 3.5 First approval
- **Emotion:** Trust test  
- **Goal:** Approve something real without fear  
- **Questions:** Will this go live? Can I edit?  
- **Messaging:** Nothing publishes without your OK (until style/trust says otherwise — always explicit)  
- **Magic:** “Approved. I’ll handle the rest.”  
- **Success:** Item approved; confidence rises  

### 3.6 Weekly usage
- **Emotion:** Habit / relief  
- **Goal:** Stay current in minutes  
- **Questions:** What’s new? Anything urgent?  
- **Messaging:** “While you were busy…” weekly package  
- **Magic:** Empty queue: “Everything looks great.”  
- **Success:** Weekly review done; owner effort stays low  

### 3.7 Monthly usage
- **Emotion:** Outcome curiosity  
- **Goal:** See if it’s working  
- **Questions:** Are we healthier? What changed?  
- **Messaging:** Marketing Health + plain-English wins  
- **Magic:** Monthly “here’s what improved”  
- **Success:** Feels ROI without spreadsheet anxiety  

### 3.8 Autonomous relationship
- **Emotion:** Trust  
- **Goal:** Less interruption, same quality  
- **Questions:** What still needs me?  
- **Messaging:** Management style + trust stage, transparent  
- **Magic:** Promotion to next trust stage  
- **Success:** Owner involvement matches chosen style  

### 3.9 Long-term customer
- **Emotion:** Partnership  
- **Goal:** Grow the business; stay visible  
- **Questions:** What’s next for *my* market?  
- **Messaging:** Outcomes, seasons, local opportunities  
- **Magic:** Anniversary / milestone trust messages  
- **Success:** Retention via peace of mind, not lock-in
