## 1. Who is speaking?

AJN Marketing speaks like an experienced Head of Marketing talking to a busy business owner:

- Professional  
- Confident  
- Calm  
- Helpful  
- Never arrogant  
- Never robotic  
- Never salesy  
- Never overwhelming  

---

## 2. Voice principles

| Do | Don’t |
|---|---|
| Plain English | Jargon (pipelines, detectors, embeddings, “synergy”) |
| Short sentences | Walls of text |
| Specific next step | Vague “optimize your funnel” |
| Honest limits | Fake guarantees of AI ranking |
| “I / we handled…” | “The system executed job…” |
| Reassure | Shame (“you’re behind”) |
| Outcome language | Feature dumping |

---

## 3. Pronouns & posture

- Prefer **“I”** for the Head-of-Marketing persona in customer-facing microcopy (“I drafted…”, “I’ll watch…”).  
- Prefer **“we”** for company commitments (guarantee, billing, legal).  
- Never talk down. Never perform superiority.

---

## 4. Words we prefer / avoid

**Prefer:** visibility, customers, reviews, Google profile, this week, approve, I’ll handle, healthy, taken care of, while you were busy.

**Avoid as hero language:** AI-powered, autonomous agent, next-gen, disrupt, leverage, synergy, SEO score, letter grade, “guaranteed ChatGPT ranking.”

AI may be mentioned factually (e.g. modern search landscape) without being the product pitch.

---

## 5. Emotional register by situation

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | Warm, efficient |
| Approvals | Clear, respectful of time |
| Success / health up | Quietly proud |
| Failure / disconnect | Calm ownership + exact fix |
| Sales / public site | Confident, not hypey |
| Admin ops | Precise, operational (different voice — see nav/admin philosophy) |

---

## 6. Microcopy patterns

- **Empty good:** “Everything looks great.”  
- **Empty needs work:** “Nothing here yet — I’ll prepare your first updates after setup.”  
- **Loading:** “Learning your business…” / “Preparing this week’s recommendations…”  
- **CTA:** One verb (“Approve”, “Reconnect Google”, “Create account”)  
- **Examples vs live:** Always label generated examples in demos.

---

## 7. StoryBrand alignment (public)

- **Hero:** Business owner  
- **Problem:** No time / inconsistent marketing / anxiety  
- **Guide:** AJN Marketing (Head of Marketing)  
- **Plan:** Connect → we work → you approve → we improve  
- **Success:** Marketing taken care of; business stays visible  
- **Failure to avoid:** Being invisible while competitors stay active  

---

## 8. Review checklist for future copy PRs

1. Would a tired contractor understand this in 5 seconds?  
2. Does it reduce anxiety?  
3. Is there one next step?  
4. Does it hide internal systems?  
5. Is anything overclaimed?
